State of Vermont

BEFORE THE HONORABLE JUDGE OF THE NORTH TONYMOUTH COUNTY COURT

AFFIDAVIT IN SUPPORT OF SEARCH WARRANT

I, Linda Ross, being duly sworn, depose and state:

1. I am a peace officer employed by the Carrillomouth Police Department with over 8 years of experience in criminal investigations.

2. This affidavit is submitted in support of a search warrant for the following location:
   2861 Bailey Neck, Chadside, NH 27322

3. I have probable cause to believe that evidence of assault is located at the above premises based on the following facts:

   a) On December 06, 2025, I responded to a report of assault at the incident location.

   b) During the investigation, the following evidence was discovered: suspicion of Vehicle EDR/Infotainment (018DOJ)

   c) Based on my training and experience, individuals involved in assault typically conceal evidence at their residences.

   d) I have received information from reliable sources indicating that the suspect may have returned to the residence with evidence.

4. The evidence sought includes:
   - Digital devices and storage media
   - Documents related to assault
   - Clothing and personal items
   - Financial records and correspondence

5. I believe this evidence is currently located at the premises and will be destroyed or removed if not seized immediately.

6. This affidavit is based on my personal knowledge and investigation of this case.
